Context

Founded in 1972, Sebrae is present throughout Brazil, acting as aÂ stimulating agent of entrepreneurship, helping in the competitiveness and sustainability of smallÂ business.

So many years of history has led to a portfolio of over 200 products, including support for those who want to formalize their business, business consulting, training courses and events, mostly face-to-face solutions.Â With a network of over 1800 service points across the country, the company's business model has always been based on presential services.

Although it has a strong capillarity power of service points, Sebrae has been working hard in recent years to make its digital transformation, and the team I work with is responsible for developing new fully digital services that can be used individually or as a support to our presential products.

As we began to develop new solutions, we had to rethink the way we offer our services, and the main channel for that was the institutional portal. This site receives millions of users per year organically, but with low performance, due to the very poor usability and as found in the search, too much content without the right direction, making it difficult for people to find what they were looking for.

The process

I was responsible for managing the entire process of rethinking from the scratch the ParanÃ¡ portal, a platform that consists of 3 different parts, Content, Store and Logged Area. Below I will describe some of the project stages.

Strategy and Research

The project strategy was built on the definition of 4 initial business objectives, which together set out to make the portal part of the company's business model, not just a passive channel. The next step was to identify and understand how people related to the platform, not just digital users but also customers who come to our offices, attendants and project managers.

To identify all stakeholder needs, a battery of qualitative surveys was conducted with more than 200 people from 3 groups, our customers, our call center team and internal employees. Once tabulated, this information was cross-referenced with the 4 business objectives, becoming the database for insights and improvements. To finish the process, we draw the user journey, which were divided into 6 personas.

Ideation

The first steps of the ideation stage were about the CMS, layout sketches and meetings with project managers, who would use this interface to communicate their projects to the business community.

Furher, with a high fidelity semi-navigable prototype, we conducted a usability test using the RITE method, which instead of waiting until the end of the study to gather the findings and suggest improvements, once a problem was identified, the fix was evaluated and implemented for the next participant.

As a final touch, instead of using stockphotos, we decided to use real Sebrae clients to illustrate the homepage, inviting them to an exclusive photo shoot at their own establishments. The goal was to humanize the portal by bringing real customers as social proof, also becoming a recognition action for successful clients.

Development

The development was carried out by a bidding company, which I was responsible for its briefing and homologation of the sprints.

The main difficulty in development was due to the fact that the content part of the site was built on wordpress, to give us freedom to create any type of page without the need for development, and the part of the store and logged area in angular, however, both interfaces needed to exchange information with each other.

Content

To keep content true to our social mission and relevant to the consumer as a market company, we hired a specialized content and SEO agency to update this publications and set up meta data for better search engine ranking. We managed to publish more than 700 contents, against just over 180 from the previous site.

Part of our customer support is carried out by our call center through digital channels, and with the aim of automating this process, together with another team we have developed a channel with chatbot to solve simpler issues, without the need for human assistance in these cases.

Outcome

Microinteractions

Showing attention to detail, we developed some feedback animations throughout the site, already in the first seconds the user will be impacted by a modern experience.
